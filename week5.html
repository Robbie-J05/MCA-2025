Week 5

Task 1: create a metadata Schema

this week we were asked to create a schema of 5 different metadata elements we could add to the MEI file we made from week 3
here is my schema

  • Title: the name of the piece
  • artist: the person/people the piece is credited to
  • publisher: the company that published the piece
  • encoder: the person who encoded the file
  • Producer: the persona responsible for the overall production of the piece
